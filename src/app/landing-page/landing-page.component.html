But what is BetKick?

BetKick stands as a football betting simulation platform, providing users with a risk-free environment to engage with real football data. This project allows users to explore and interact with football matches and standings, bet on game results and compete with others to see who makes the best predictions.

All The Features, Zero Risk

Filter matches by date or competition, check out live and today's games, and have fun betting on any match without the stress of losing your money.

Track Your Progress

Track every step of your journey seamlessly. Review active, live, and finished bets to learn and plan your next moves. Gain insights, learn from your successes, and strategize for future wins.

Explore the Rankings

Check out team standings in different competitions. Sort tables by stats, and click on a team's name to see their upcoming matches.

Rise to the Top

Explore the rankings and see who's dominating the game! Check out the leaderboard to discover our most successful users, or click on the columns to change the order and see the not-so successful ones.
